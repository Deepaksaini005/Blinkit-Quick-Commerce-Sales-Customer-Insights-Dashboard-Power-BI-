# 📊 Quick Commerce Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

🚀 This project presents a **complete business intelligence solution** for a quick commerce platform (similar to Blinkit/Zepto), built using **Power BI**.  
It analyzes **sales, customers, products, delivery performance, and marketing ROI** to uncover actionable insights.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard helps in understanding:

- 📈 Revenue trends & seasonal patterns  
- 🛒 Customer behavior & retention  
- 📦 Product performance  
- 🚚 Delivery efficiency  
- 💰 Marketing ROI & profitability  

---

## 📊 Key Dashboards

### 1️⃣ Sales & Revenue Analysis
- Total Revenue: **4.97M+**
- Monthly revenue trends (peak: Aug–Oct)
- Category-wise revenue breakdown
- Revenue vs Orders correlation

👉 Insight:
- Strong growth observed mid-year  
- Drop in November–December  

---

### 2️⃣ Customer Insights
- Total Customers: **2172**
- Retention Rate: **68.69%**
- Repeat Customers: **1492**

#### Segments:
- High Value, Medium Value, Low Value customers

👉 Insight:
- Majority customers are repeat users  
- High-value segment contributes major revenue  

---

### 3️⃣ Product & Category Analysis
- Top Categories:  
  - Dairy & Breakfast  
  - Pharmacy  
  - Fruits & Vegetables  

- Top Products:
  - Vitamins  
  - Toilet Cleaner  

👉 Insight:
- Essential goods dominate sales  
- Some products generate low revenue → optimization needed  

---

### 4️⃣ Delivery Performance Analysis
- Avg Delivery Time: **287 sec**
- On-Time Delivery: **35%**
- Late Delivery Rate: **64.89%**

👉 Insight:
- Delivery system needs major improvement  
- High delay impacts customer satisfaction  

---

### 5️⃣ Marketing & Profitability Analysis
- Total Marketing Spend: **16.32M**
- Total Revenue: **11.01M**
- Profit: **-11.35M**
- ROI: **-228%**

👉 Insight:
- Heavy losses due to over-spending  
- Marketing strategy needs optimization
